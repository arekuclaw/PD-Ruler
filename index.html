Measure pupillary distance from a photo using a driver license as scale.

1 · Add a photo

Take a straight-on photo of the face with a driver license (or any ID-1 card) held flat against the forehead or cheek — in the same plane as the eyes, parallel to the camera. Look directly at the lens.

Hold the ID card flat in the same plane as your eyes, parallel to the lens, and look straight ahead.

2 · Adjust markers
